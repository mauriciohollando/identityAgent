Stop handoffs and payouts you haven’t earned the right to trust

Trust Auditor is the agent handoff gate: an A2A-native API that helps you allow, deny, or escalate for review before you delegate work, call another agent, or release funds. It combines identity signals (optional registry), behavioral history (your transaction outcomes), and structured evidence—so policy and compliance aren’t guessing.

Who it’s for first: small technical teams (often seed–Series A) shipping B2B or prosumer agents that move money or high-impact actions—not “generic AI trust” for everyone.

View pricing Source & docs

Product truth: Reputation numbers come from your (or your partners’) transaction logs when you connect an MCP-style history service. Without that, the stack can run in demo / stub mode—fine for integration tests, not for production buyer decisions. See Getting started (configure config.js with your GitHub URL).

How the gate works

  1. Log what actually happened — Your platform records success, failure, refunded, etc., into the transaction log (or compatible MCP sources).
  2. Audit before handoff — On the path to delegation or payout, call the auditor with target_agent_id and context; optional registries add identity attestations.
  3. Enforce your policy — You get APPROVED / FLAGGED / REVIEW_REQUIRED, a trust score as an input (not a substitute for your rules), warnings, and evidence for audits.
