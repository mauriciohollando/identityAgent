How checkout works

  1. Create Stripe Payment Links (or Checkout links) for each price in the live Stripe Dashboard.
  2. Paste those URLs into reputation-auditor-site/config.js under stripePaymentLinks.
  3. Deploy this static site. Buttons open Stripe; after payment, ensure your Stripe Customer is mapped to your hiring_agent_id on the payment adapter (see repo docs).

Until links are configured, buttons fall back to email so you never ship secret keys in the browser.
